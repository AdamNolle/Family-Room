# 🏠 Family Room

### 🚧 Work in Progress
This app is currently in active development. Features are being added and refined frequently.

--- 

> **A Private Home for Your Family's Memories.**

**Family Room** is a native Apple application designed to help you organize and store your family's most precious photos and videos. Built with privacy at its core, it uses on-device AI to help you find the people and moments that matter most, all while keeping your data securely synced through iCloud.

---

## ✨ Features
- **🧠 On-Device Intelligence**: Facial recognition and grouping performed entirely on your device.
- **☁️ Private iCloud Sync**: Your library stays in sync across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
- **👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 People Circle**: A dedicated space for the faces that matter.
